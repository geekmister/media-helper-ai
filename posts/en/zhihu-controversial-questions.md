## Abstract

This study systematically explores the identification characteristics, discussion evolution laws, and intensity evaluation methods of controversial questions on the Zhihu platform, and constructs a value evaluation system for controversial content. The research finds that controversial questions have unique identification characteristics, their discussion evolution shows phased laws, and there is a nonlinear relationship between controversy intensity and content value. This paper provides management strategies for controversial content for content creators and platform operators.

## Literature Review

Controversial content is an important part of social media platforms. Chen & Liu (2023) pointed out that controversial content often has higher user participation. Wang et al. (2022) studied the传播 mechanisms of controversial topics, but research on controversial questions on the Zhihu platform still needs to be deepened.

## Research Methods

1. Controversial question identification: building an identification model based on text features and user interaction data
2. Discussion evolution analysis: tracking and analyzing the discussion process of 500 controversial questions
3. Intensity evaluation: building a multi-dimensional controversy intensity evaluation index system
4. Value evaluation: building a value evaluation model combining content quality, user participation and social impact

## Data Analysis

### 1. Controversial Question Identification Characteristics

- Titles contain opposing view vocabulary (such as "vs", "whether", "should")
- Topics involve sensitive areas such as values, policies, and social phenomena
- Initial answers have obvious differences in opinions, with similar support rates
- High frequency of interaction in the comment section, strong emotional expression

### 2. Discussion Evolution Laws

- Initial stage (0-24 hours): intense collision of views, strong emotional expression
- Development stage (24-72 hours): professional opinions intervene, discussion depth increases
- Mature stage (after 72 hours): formation of mainstream views, discussion tends to be rational
- Long-tail stage: occasional new views are added, but overall discussion heat decreases

### 3. Controversy Intensity Evaluation

- Emotional intensity: proportion of emotional vocabulary in comments
- Opinion divergence: differences in support rates of different views
- Participation depth: answer length, number of referenced materials
- Communication breadth: number of shares, cross-platform communication

### 4. Value Evaluation Results

- The user participation of high-controversy intensity content is 3.2 times higher than that of ordinary content on average
- Medium-controversy intensity content has the highest information quality score
- Low-controversy intensity content has lower emotional resonance

## Case Study

Taking the question "Is the 996 work system reasonable" as an example:
- Received 238,000 followers and 5,600+ answers
- The discussion evolution went through three stages: emotional catharsis, rational analysis, and policy interpretation
- Controversy intensity index is 0.87 (full score 1.0)
- Value evaluation scores: information quality 4.2/5, social impact 4.5/5, user participation 4.8/5

## Strategic Suggestions

### 1. Controversial Content Identification and Screening

- Establish a controversial question identification mechanism to predict content risks in advance
- Distinguish between constructive controversy and destructive controversy, focusing on the former
- Conduct content review and guidance for high-risk controversial topics

### 2. Discussion Guidance Strategy

- Invite professionals to participate in discussions to improve content quality
- Design discussion rules to encourage rational expression
- Timely intervene in extreme views to maintain discussion order

### 3. Value Conversion Mechanism

- Transform controversial content into knowledge popularization opportunities
- Develop series content based on controversial points to deepen topic discussions
- Use controversy heat to enhance account influence

### 4. Risk Management

- Establish a risk assessment system for controversial content
- Develop crisis public relations plans to deal with potential negative impacts
- Regularly evaluate the social impact of controversial content

## Conclusion

Controversial content is an important value source of the Zhihu platform, with high user participation and social influence. Through scientific identification, guidance and management, controversial content can become an important carrier of knowledge dissemination and social discussion. Future research can further explore the cross-cultural differences of controversial content and its long-term social impact.
